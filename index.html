Research Experience | 研究经验

Quantum Heat Engine Model of Photosynthetic Reaction Center (on going)
光合作用反应中心的量子热机模型(进行中)

Mathematics Behind the Standard Model (2019)
Derived Klein-Gordon, Dirac, and Proca Equation form symmetries and groups, including particles interaction and the origin of mass.
标准模型后的数学(2019)
从对称性和群论导出克莱因-戈尔登、狄拉克、普罗喀方程，也包括了粒子相互作用及质量的来源。

Asteroid Photometry (2018)
Measured light curves for Arthurmiller, Gantrisch, Mikawa, Oishi, Rubtsov, 1990 US4, and 1999 JX37.
小行星光曲线测量(2018)
测量了上列出的小行星的光曲线。

Hydrogen Wave Equation (2018)
Solved Schrödinger's equation for the hydrogen atom with ideas in partial differential equations, and examined the well posedness of the solution.
氢原子波函数(2018)
利用偏微分方程的知识得到氢原子薛定谔方程的解，并研究了其的适定性。

Quantum Game Theory (2018)
Researched quantum games, specifically on quantum prisoner’s dilemma.
量子博弈论(2018)
研究了量子囚徒困境问题。

Quantum Entanglement and Application (2018)
Researched entanglement and applications such as quantum teleportation and superdense coding.
量子纠缠及应用(2018)
研究了量子纠缠以及其在量子隐形传态和超密编码的应用。

Determination of Orbital Elements of an Asteroid (2017)
Determined orbital elements of an asteroid with existing photos using Maple and MATLAB.
判定小行星轨道参数(2017)
利用数张小行星照片，使用Maple和MATLAB判定其轨道参数。

Determination of the Band-gap of Semiconductors (2017)
Used an op-amp circuit with an Arduino board to determine the Band-gap of different semiconductors.
判定半导体能隙(2017)
利用运放和Arduino判定不同半导体能隙。

Numerical Method for Electric Potential Field (2016)
Used numerical methods to give approximate solutions to a boundary value problem of electric potential.
电势场的数值法(2017)
利用数值法解电势场的边值问题。
