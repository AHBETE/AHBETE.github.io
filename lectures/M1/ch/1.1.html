What is mechanics? Mechanics is the study deal with motion (kinematics) and the action of forces (dynamics).

什么是力学? 力学是研究运动 (运动学) 和力的作用 (动力学) 的学科.

Mathematical models can be constructed to simulate real-life situations.

数学模型可以被建立来模拟现实场景.

However, in many cases, the problem would be too complicated. Therefore, assumptions may be applied to simplify the problem.

然而, 很多情况下, 问题会过于复杂, 因此可以用一些假设来简化问题.
