Common models and assumptions:

常见的模型和假设:

Models
模型		 Assumptions
假设
Particle
质点		 Mass is concentrated at a single point.
质量集中于一点.
Rotational force and air resistance can be ignored.
转动的力和空气阻力可以被忽略.
Uniform
(质量)分布均匀		 Mass can be considered to be concentrated at the geometric center - the center of mass.
质量可以视作集中于几何中心 - 质心.
Light
轻的		 Mass can be ignored.
质量可以被忽略.
Inextensible string
不可延伸的绳子		 Acceleration is the same on both ends.
不可延伸, 绳子两端的加速度一致.
Smooth
光滑		 No friction.
不计摩擦.
