A vector is a quantity which has both magnitude and direction.

矢量(向量)是一个同时具有大小和方向的量.

A scalar quantity has magnitude only.

标量只具有大小.

In this textbook, when considering motion problems a 1-D vector can be both positive and negative, but a scalar quantities is always positive.

在这本教材中, 当考虑运动问题时, 一个一维矢量可以是正的也可以是负的, 但是标量总是是正的.

2.3.1 Notation
标记

Column vector 2D:

列向量(二维):

\begin{equation*} \boldsymbol{p}=\begin{pmatrix}a\\b\end{pmatrix}. \end{equation*}

The above equation means that, vector $ \boldsymbol{p}$ has $x$-component $a$, and $y$-component $b$.

上式表达的是, 矢量$ \boldsymbol{p}$的平行于$x$-轴方向分量为$a$, 平行于$y$-轴方向分量为$b$. (注意: 教材、讲义和课件中, 通常用加粗表示矢量, 板书和笔记中则用类似$\vec{p}$来标记).

A unit vector has length 1. The unit vectors along the Cartesian axes are usually denoted by $\hat{\imath}$ and $\hat{\jmath}$.

单位矢量长度为1. 平行于笛卡尔坐标系(平面直角坐标系)的坐标轴的单位矢量通常记作$\hat{\imath}$和$\hat{\jmath}$.

$\hat{\imath}$ and $\hat{\jmath}$ notation:

$\hat{\imath}$和$\hat{\jmath}$标记:

有了单位矢量$\hat{\imath}$和$\hat{\jmath}$, 矢量$ \boldsymbol{p}$可以这样表示

\begin{equation*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}. \end{equation*}

2.3.2 Vector Algebra
矢量运算

Addition/subtraction 加法/减法 Addition can be completed by adding the terms in $\hat{\imath}$ and $\hat{\jmath}$ separately. I.e., 加法可以通过将带$\hat{\imath}$的项相加, 在将带$\hat{\jmath}$的项相加完成. (对$\hat{\imath}$和$\hat{\jmath}$分量分别相加). 即: \begin{gather*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}, \boldsymbol{q}=c\hat{\imath}+d\hat{\jmath}, \boldsymbol{p}+ \boldsymbol{q}=(a+c)\hat{\imath}+(b+d)\hat{\jmath}. \end{gather*} Subtraction can be completed similarly. 减法也类似. \begin{figure}[!h] \centering \includegraphics[width=0.45\textwidth]{figure/vecadd.eps} \caption{Vector addition. 向量加法.} %\label{fig:my_label} \end{figure} Multiplication to a scalar/division by a scalar 乘/除以一个标量 \begin{gather*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}, e \boldsymbol{p}=(ae)\hat{\imath}+(be)\hat{\jmath}. \end{gather*} \subsection{Magnitude and Direction} {\Large 2.3.3 大小(模)和方向} Magnitude of a vector can be found using Pythagoras' theorem, 矢量的大小(模)可以通过勾股定理来求, \begin{gather*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}, {| \boldsymbol{p}|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}.} \end{gather*} Direction of a vector can be found using trigonometry. For example, the direction of a vector with respect to positive $x$-axis direction is 矢量的方向可以用三角函数来求. 例如, 一个矢量和$x$轴正方向的夹角$\theta$是 \begin{gather*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}, {\theta=\tan^{-1}\left(\frac{b}{a}\right).} \end{gather*} \textbf{Bearing} is a direction measured from north and it tracks angle in clockwise direction with north line which means north represents 0 degree, east is 90 degrees, south is 180 degrees and west is 270 degrees. \textbf{方向角}描述了相对北方($y$-轴正方向)所呈的顺时针的角度, 即正北为0度, 正东是90度, 正南是180度, 正西是270度. \begin{figure}[!h] \centering \includegraphics[width=0.45\textwidth]{figure/bearing.eps} \caption{Bearing. 方向角.} %\label{fig:my_label} \end{figure} \subsection{Parallel Vectors} {\Large 2.3.4 平行矢量} It is not hard to see that an arbitrary vector, say $ \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}$ is parallel to its scalar multiplication, for example $e \boldsymbol{p}=(ae)\hat{\imath}+(be)\hat{\jmath}$, as their direction are the same 不难看出, 任意一矢量, 比如$ \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}$和它的标量积结果, 比如$e \boldsymbol{p}=(ae)\hat{\imath}+(be)\hat{\jmath}$, 是平行的, 因为他们的方向一致 \begin{equation*} \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{ae}{be}\right)=\tan^{-1}\left(\frac{a}{b}\right). \end{equation*} It is not hard to see that, [the ratio of $\hat{\imath}$-component of $ \boldsymbol{p}$ and that of $e \boldsymbol{p}$] is same as [the ratio of $\hat{\jmath}$-component of $ \boldsymbol{p}$ and that of $e \boldsymbol{p}$]. Then in general, for an arbitrary set of parallel vectors, the ratio of their $\hat{\imath}$-component should be same as the ratio of their $\hat{\jmath}$-component. 不难看出, [$ \boldsymbol{p}$的$\hat{\imath}$分量与$e \boldsymbol{p}$的$\hat{\imath}$的比值]与[$ \boldsymbol{p}$的$\hat{\jmath}$分量与$e \boldsymbol{p}$的$\hat{\jmath}$的比值]相等. 类推可得, 任意一对平行向量, 它们的$\hat{\imath}$分量的比值应该等于$\hat{\jmath}$分量的比值. From here, we can conclude that, for a set of parallel vectors, if there are parallel, then we have 继而便有结论, 若一对向量平行, 则有 \begin{gather*} \boldsymbol{p}=a\hat{\imath}+b\hat{\jmath}, \boldsymbol{q}=c\hat{\imath}+d\hat{\jmath}, \text{If } \boldsymbol{p}\parallel \boldsymbol{q}, {\text{then }\frac{a}{c}=\frac{b}{d}, \text{ or }\frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d}.} \end{gather*} Special case: when a vector is parallel to $\hat{\imath}$, then its $\hat{\jmath}$-component (coefficient of $\hat{\jmath}$) is zero; or when a vector is parallel to $\hat{\jmath}$, then its $\hat{\imath}$-component (coefficient of $\hat{\imath}$) is zero. 特殊情况: 当一个矢量和$\hat{\imath}$平行, 则它的$\hat{\jmath}$分量($\hat{\jmath}$的系数)是零; 当一个矢量和$\hat{\jmath}$平行, 则它的$\hat{\imath}$分量($\hat{\imath}$的系数)是零.
