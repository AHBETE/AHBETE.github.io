耶鲁大学Shelly Kagen对于灵不存在的论证：

  1. Only compostie things can be destroyed.
  2. Only changing things are composite.
  3. Invisible things don't change.
  4. (so) Invisible things cannot be destroyed.
  5. (but) Soal is innvisible.
  6. (so) Soul can't be destroyed.
